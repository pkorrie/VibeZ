About VibeZ

In today's world, we could all use some positivity. Vibez, a cutting-edge social media platform, allows you to send good, bad, and immaculate vibes to the posts of peers. Based on vibe check ratings, a mood ring appears after a post reaches a certain threshold of likes to draw attention to the good in this world. With users on this platform posting fit checks, fresh memes, motivations, devotions, small business support, outdoor adventures, or even their daily boring tasks, members have the opportunity to learn how others view their vibe. Vibez... put some good back in this world.

How to Use VibeZ

The pink menu button can be used to navigate between your profile, the post feed, and the info page. This button can be dragged around to a position that is more comfortable for you. The post page will allow you to see posts from the community and reply to posts, as well as liking the posts. Depending on how many likes a post has, it will have a dazzling border to show that the degree to which the post is "Vibing." To see replies to posts, click on the view reply button towards the bottom of the post. To add a post, go to the top of the page and add a title, type in your thoughts, and optionally choose a picture to go with your thoughts! The profile page will show your first name, last name, profile picture, bio, and email. You can update your information by clicking the "Edit" button. To search for other user's profiles, click the magnifying glass symbol on the top right of the page, enter in their user name, and it will bring you to their profile page! To use the chat room feature, click on the chat room button located on the bottom right of the screen. You can chat with other users without having to send posts! At this time all users will see the messages. Thank you for being a part of the Vibez community!
